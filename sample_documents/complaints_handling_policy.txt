Section 1: Purpose

This policy defines the process for receiving, investigating, and resolving customer complaints.

Section 2: Complaint Registration

Every complaint must be assigned a complaint reference number and recorded with the customer name, contact information, complaint category, date received, and responsible department.

Section 3: Acknowledgement

The customer must receive an acknowledgement within two business days after the complaint is registered.

Section 4: Investigation

The responsible employee must review the customer communication, transaction history, supporting documents, and applicable policies.

Section 5: Resolution

The complaint should normally be resolved within fifteen business days. If additional investigation is required, the customer must receive a status update.

Section 6: Escalation

Complaints involving fraud, discrimination, regulatory concerns, or potential legal action must be escalated to the appropriate risk or compliance team.
