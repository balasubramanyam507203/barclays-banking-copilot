Section 1: Purpose

This policy describes the minimum customer identity verification requirements.

Section 2: Individual Customers

Employees must verify the customer's full legal name, date of birth, residential address, and an approved government-issued identification document.

Section 3: Corporate Customers

For corporate customers, employees must verify the registered business name, business address, registration number, authorized representatives, and beneficial owners.

Section 4: Failed Verification

If the identity information cannot be verified, the employee must not complete the requested account activity. The case must be escalated to the compliance team.

Section 5: Sensitive Information

Customer identification information must not be copied into ordinary application logs or shared with unauthorized employees.
